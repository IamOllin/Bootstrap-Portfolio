About


I’m Hugo, a UX/UI designer passionate about user-centered design. I leverage iterative testing and user research to refine existing interfaces, ensuring they align with current trends. I bring a historical perspective, drawing from past design principles to inform current and future brand strategy.


My passion for design extends beyond UX/UI. Previously, I explored artistic expression through public mural research in my MA graduate program, which instilled a foundation for visual communication and helped me continue a saying I was told from a young age by my mother and father “sigue adelante”.
